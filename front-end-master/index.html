Overview of Consulting Services

All services provided by Green Candle Consulting center around crypto & NFTs. We have a focus on crypto gaming since it is a key driver of crypto mass adoption. We will deliver specific capabilities to ensure our customers receive support in all phases of their organization’s / project’s life cycle from strategy & investment via implementation & operation to learning & development. Key service offerings are designed and delivered by subject matter experts & coordinated by experienced delivery managers:

Crypto & NFT Project Advisory Services

Consulting on setup and growth of crypto and NFT projects from inception to operation: Strategy, Finance, Marketing, and Technology.

Trend Analysis and Portfolio Management

Build a trader’s mindset, understanding current / future trends in the industry: Provide key strategies within personalized risk profile, what we call mastering ‘cryptovestment’.

Crypto Acceleration

Enable organizations to adopt a “crypto mindset” effectively and efficiently through learning and engagement: Get you up and running in all-things crypto

Web3 Development

Consult on the importance of Web3 and how it can be incorporated into your existing business model: Explore taking a venture into the metaverse.

Target Markets and Growth Research

We primarily focus on Web3 Project Teams, Crytpo/NFT Funds, For-Profit Companies, and Future Growth Opportunities.

Security and Risk Management

Deliver proactive and reactive strategies to keep your crypto assets safe and secure: Share industry best practices and become the keeper of your own keys
