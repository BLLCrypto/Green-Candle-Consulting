Overview of Consulting Services

The management and technology consulting services provided by Green Candle Consulting center around Web3. We deliver specific capabilities to ensure our customers receive support in all phases of their organization’s / project’s life cycle from strategy & investment via implementation to operation. Services are provided by subject matter experts & coordinated by experienced delivery managers:

Web3 Project Advisory Services

Consult on setup and growth of Web3 projects from inception to operation

We advise across various sectors including defi, scaling solutions and gaming

Web3 Acceleration

We help businesses strategize on how to use Web3 for scaling

We are there all the way from knowledge development, strategy definition, implementation and operations support

Financial Planning & Fund-Raising Support

We define income streams, value businesses, plan financials & provide ongoing support for investment rounds

We work together with entities specialized in creating investment leads

Marketing, Brand & Positioning

Assess current positioning and recommend adjustments to get product ‘on message’ for target groups

We explore perception of the project from both investor and end user point of view

Project Audits | Broad or Limited Scope

We audit projects with the aim to resolve particular challenges, e.g. increasing runway, or to check general project health

Our assessments serve as the basis of a strategy exercise or concrete implementation measures

Security and Risk Management

Deliver proactive and reactive strategies to keep your crypto assets safe and secure: Share industry best practices and become the keeper of your own keys.
